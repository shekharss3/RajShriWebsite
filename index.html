About RajShri-Electricals

Founded in 1995, RajShri-Electricals has provided professional manufacturing, installation and maintenance service to clients throughout the north part of India for over 24 years. With office in jaipur,being in heart of rajasthan, with a team of highly skilled flexible workers we can provide efficient, cost-effective and timely panel manufacturing and manitenance service for your next project.
